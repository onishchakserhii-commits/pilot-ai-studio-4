# **App Name**: Pilot AI Studio

## Core Features:

- Multilingual Website Structure: Implements i18n for French, English, and Ukrainian content, with an extensible architecture to easily add German and Italian in the future, covering all site pages and key messages.
- Lead Capture & Free Audit Forms: Comprehensive, user-friendly forms for contact, free audit requests, and service inquiries, designed with specific fields (name, business name, website/Google Maps link, email, phone/WhatsApp, message) to maximize lead conversion.
- Dynamic Service & Case Study Display: Dedicated sections utilizing cards and clear layouts to present specific business categories (e.g., restaurants, salons), problem/solution scenarios, detailed services (website creation/improvement, AI chatbot, n8n automation, local audit), pricing packages, and illustrative portfolio cases.
- AI Chatbot Client Tool: Integrates an AI-powered conversational tool to provide immediate answers to common customer questions, offer preliminary consultations on services, and efficiently collect lead information or schedule appointments.
- N8N Automation Integration: Configures webhooks for all form submissions to connect seamlessly with n8n, enabling automated workflows for lead management (e.g., sending data to CRM/Google Sheets/WhatsApp), notifications, appointment reminders, and automated Google review requests.
- Google Ecosystem Preparedness: Includes robust implementation of Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Tag Manager (GTM), along with essential SEO elements, to ensure readiness for Google Search Console (GSC), integration of Google Business Profile links, and optimization for Google Ads campaigns.
- Responsive & Mobile-First UI: Delivers a clean, modern, and adaptive user interface optimized for mobile devices, featuring large, legible headings, ample negative space ('much air'), neat content blocks, and soft, subtle animations to enhance user experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, muted blue-grey (#477085) to convey trust, professionalism, and a modern technological aesthetic, chosen to stand out clearly against a light background.
- Background color: A very light, subtle cool grey (#F0F3F4) that provides an airy, clean, and premium canvas for content, ensuring readability and a minimalist feel.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear blue (#2662D9) analogous to the primary, used to draw attention to call-to-action buttons, key interactive elements, and reinforce the technological, results-driven aspect of the service.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its techy, modern, yet clear aesthetic. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for exceptional readability across devices and screen sizes, ensuring simple and understandable text for all users.
- Minimal, crisp, and functional line icons that align with the clean, Swiss-inspired design. Icons should be clear, intuitive, and complement the professional, trustworthy feel without adding visual clutter.
- A layout characterized by ample whitespace ('much air'), structured content blocks, and large, impactful headings. Emphasis on 'cards' for services and simple process diagrams, all optimized with a mobile-first approach for intuitive navigation.
- Subtle and soft animations (e.g., gentle fade-ins on scroll, smooth transitions for interactive elements) to provide an engaging yet professional user experience, implemented with Framer Motion for fluidity.
